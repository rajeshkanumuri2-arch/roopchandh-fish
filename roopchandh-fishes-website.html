About Roopchandh Fishes

Roopchandh Fishes is a family-run aquaculture business focused on responsible fish production. We combine traditional knowledge with modern water-quality monitoring and biosecurity to increase yields while reducing environmental impact.

Our approach

  • Selective broodstock and disease screening
  • Site-specific pond design and water management
  • Farmer training and cooperative marketing
  • Traceability from hatchery to buyer

Water Quality — Quick Checklist

  • pH: 6.5 — 8.5
  • Dissolved Oxygen: >5 mg/L (aeration recommended below 5 mg/L)
  • Ammonia: < 0.02 mg/L (total ammonia nitrogen)
  • Regular sludge removal & periodic pond drying

Need lab-quality tests? Ask about our water testing kits and monthly monitoring plans.
